Why GamePetition?

We help player communities organize around features, balance, server issues, mod support, and policy changes — and deliver those petitions directly to the teams that can act.

Unique Selling Points (USPs)

  • Real-time Signature Tracking: Live counters and growth graphs for every petition.
  • Verified Gamer Accounts: Link profiles from Steam, Xbox, PlayStation, or Discord to improve credibility.
  • Game-Specific Templates: Pre-built petition templates for bugs, features, servers, monetisation complaints, and more.
  • Built-in Social Sharing: One-click sharing to Twitter, Discord, Reddit, and Facebook with optimized previews.
  • Developer Outreach Tools: Send curated reports and signature summaries directly to developer contacts.
  • In-Game Overlay Support: Optional overlays show petition progress while playing (where supported).
  • Powerful Analytics: Demographics, geo-heatmaps, referral tracking, and signature quality scoring.
  • Fraud Detection & Moderation: Anti-bot checks, rate limits, and moderation workflows to keep petitions legitimate.
  • GDPR & Privacy First: Clear consent, data controls, and exportable records for compliance.
  • Multi-language Support: Localize petitions and landing pages for global player bases.
  • Mobile-First Experience: Responsive signing flows and push-ready share cards for mobile users.
  • Custom Branding: White-label petitions with logos, colors, and domains for communities and influencers.
  • Webhook & API Integrations: Connect signatures to CRMs, analytics, and community tools.
  • Pre-Verified Signatures: Optional gating by platform account to increase developer trust.
  • Email Campaigns & Alerts: Notify signers, donors, and stakeholders when petitions hit milestones.
  • Signature Milestones & Gamification: Badges, goals, and progress bars to motivate supporters.
  • Exportable Evidence Packs: Download curated packets (signatures, comments, analytics) to send with formal requests.
  • Community Moderation: Trusted moderators can vet and promote high-quality petitions.
  • Easy Embedded Widgets: Place sign-up widgets or progress bars on external sites and forums.
  • Low Friction UX: Fast sign flow (name + email) with optional verified account add-ons.

How It Works

  1. Create: Choose a template, write your ask, and target the right developer or studio.
  2. Share: Use built-in social tools, widgets, and integrations to reach players across platforms.
  3. Collect: Track real-time signatures, comments, and supporter data.
  4. Deliver: Generate an evidence pack and send it to the developer with clear, measurable demands.
  5. Follow Up: Use email campaigns and updates to keep momentum and pressure.
