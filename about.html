About GamePetition

GamePetition was born from a simple idea: give players a united voice and a structured way to communicate with game developers. Whether you're petitioning for a much‑needed bug fix, a new feature, or a change in policy, our platform empowers communities to organize, share, and deliver their demands effectively.

Our small team of gamers, designers, and developers understands the frustration that comes when requests are ignored. We built GamePetition to bridge that gap with modern tools, verified accounts, and analytics that help petitions be taken seriously.

We believe in transparency, fairness, and player power. Every signature you collect is yours — you control the data, and we provide the platform to amplify your message.

Get in touch if you'd like to collaborate, suggest improvements, or just say hello.
